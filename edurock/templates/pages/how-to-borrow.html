How to Borrow a Book

Borrowing Process

Our library makes it easy for members to access thousands of titles. Simply browse the catalogue, check for availability, and present your Membership ID at the circulation desk for a quick checkout.

Borrowing Rules

To ensure a fair experience for all readers, please keep the following guidelines in mind:

  • Standard borrowing period is 14 days (2 weeks).
  • Books can be renewed once if there are no pending reservations.
  • Membership ID card is mandatory for every exchange.
  • Please check for any existing damages before leaving the desk.
  • Late returns incur a fee as per your membership agreement.

Can't Find a Book?

If a specific title is not available in our current catalogue, you can use the request form to the right. Our procurement team will review your request and notify you once the book is added to our shelves.

  • Request specific authors or new releases.
  • Get notified immediately via email upon arrival.
