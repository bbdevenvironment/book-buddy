Help & Support

Member Knowledge Base

Everything you need to know about borrowing, memberships, and library guidelines.

faq

Applying is easy! You can choose a Monthly, Half-Yearly, or Annual plan from our membership page. Once payment is confirmed, your Membership ID will be generated, which is mandatory for all book exchanges.

Books can be kept for a period of 2 weeks. You may renew the book once if no other member has reserved it. Please ensure returns are made within one month to avoid late charges.

We take pride in our collection. Scribbling, tearing, or any form of damage is not allowed. If a book is damaged while in your care, the cost of replacing the book will be charged to your account.

The number of books specified in your membership plan denotes the maximum count of books you can borrow during a single visit. For example, a "3-Book Plan" allows you to have 3 books at home simultaneously.

Memberships should be renewed within 2 days from the Membership End Date. This grace period helps us manage library operations effectively and ensures you have uninterrupted access to our collection.
